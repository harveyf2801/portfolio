Information

In the following tests, you will be provided with a reference track and a series of test tracks. Please evaluate each test track based on how its perceived loudness and quality compare to the reference track. Play the reference track first before every test track, and ensure that you listen to each track in full before providing your evaluation.

On the loudness scale, -10 would be interpreted as the test track being significantly quieter than the reference, 0 would mean there was no difference between the two, and +10 would represent a significant increase in loudness.

When evaluating quality, -10 represents a significant decline in quality (for example: strange artifacts, noise, or distortion have occurred). Again, 0 represents no changes, and +10 would be interpreted as the quality being significantly improved.
